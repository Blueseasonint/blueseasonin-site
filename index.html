Who We Are

A diversified private investment and business development company with a long-term perspective.

Founded in Israel in 2004, Blue Season International Ltd. is a privately owned investment and business development company wholly owned by entrepreneur and investor Guy Koubbi.

Over the past two decades, the company has maintained continuous business activity across a broad range of industries, combining entrepreneurial experience, strategic thinking, and long-term commitment.

Blue Season's philosophy is based on identifying opportunities with strong fundamentals, supporting capable management teams, and building sustainable value through disciplined execution and responsible growth.

Today, the company continues to participate in ventures, investments, and strategic partnerships both in Israel and internationally.

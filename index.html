Who We Are

A diversified private investment and business development company with a long-term perspective.

Founded in Israel in 2004, Blue Season International Ltd. is a privately owned investment and business development company wholly owned by entrepreneur and investor Guy Koubbi.

Over the past two decades, the company has maintained continuous business activity across a broad range of industries, combining entrepreneurial experience, strategic thinking, and long-term commitment.

Blue Season's philosophy is based on identifying opportunities with strong fundamentals, supporting capable management teams, and building sustainable value through disciplined execution and responsible growth.

Today, the company continues to participate in ventures, investments, and strategic partnerships both in Israel and internationally.

A diversified business platform built on experience, relationships, and long-term value creation.

Blue Season maintains an entrepreneurial mindset while focusing on opportunities supported by strong fundamentals, capable people, and strategic partnerships.

Our Journey

More than two decades of continuous business activity, development, and diversification.

2004

Blue Season International Ltd. is established

The company begins its operations in Israel, focusing on the import, marketing, and distribution of premium alcoholic beverages.

2004–2015

Building commercial foundations

Blue Season develops expertise in international trade, brand development, marketing, distribution networks, and commercial operations.

2015

Expansion into new sectors

The company expands beyond trade and distribution and begins diversifying into additional sectors and investment opportunities.

2015–Present

Diversification and strategic growth

Blue Season broadens its interests across real estate, fuel station ventures, land investments, industrial generator projects, technology startups, alternative investments, and strategic partnerships.

2024–2025

New investment platforms

Development and expansion of new strategic ventures, including activities related to alternative investments, whisky casks, and international business initiatives.

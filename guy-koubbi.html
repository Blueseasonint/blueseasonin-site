Entrepreneur, Investor & Business Developer

Guy Koubbi is an entrepreneur, investor, and business developer with more than two decades of experience across multiple industries and business sectors.

Since founding Blue Season International Ltd. in 2004, he has participated in the establishment, growth, and development of numerous ventures and strategic initiatives in Israel and internationally.

Over the years, he has served in various capacities including founder, shareholder, director, strategic partner, and investor, contributing to companies and ventures operating across a diverse range of industries.

His business philosophy emphasizes long-term value creation, disciplined execution, strategic partnerships, and responsible growth.
